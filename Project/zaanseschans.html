Zaanse Schans

Zaanse Schans lures you with its charm and cultural richness, and the best part? It's all yours to explore absolutely free. This destination is a must-visit for tourists eager to immerse themselves in the traditional Zaanse culture.

Wind mills

Step into a historical era as you encounter the iconic windmills that have graced Dutch history books for centuries. Dating back to the 18th century, these magnificent structures were far more than picturesque ornaments. They once served practical functions, grinding grain and cutting wood. Today, they stand as a testament to Dutch craftsmanship and cultural heritage, inviting you to witness history come alive.

Henri Willig

The Netherlands is known for its cheese, and at Zaanse Schans, you'll discover Henri Willig, a haven for some of the finest Dutch cheeses. Here, you can not only purchase these delectable cheeses but also witness skilled artisans at work, fashioning the delicious creations that have made Dutch cheese world-famous.

Zaansmuseum

Next to the Zaanse Schans lies the Zaansmuseum, a treasure filled with history. This museum offers an immersive journey through the rich heritage of the Zaanstreek region. Engaging storytelling and informative exhibits transport you through time, unveiling the remarkable craftsmanship and storied history that define the Zaan area. Prepare to uncover the extraordinary history that shaped this enchanting region.
