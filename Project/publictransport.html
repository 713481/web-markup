Public Transport

Navigating Zaandam's public transport system is straightforward and convenient, catering to both tourists and visitors from other cities.

Bus services

Exploring Zaandam via bus is a breeze. The well-organized bus services offer efficient routes that swiftly transport you to your desired destination. For your convenience, you can plan your journey using the official Connexxion website to find the fastest route to your final stop.

Connexxion official website

Train services

If your journey takes you beyond Zaandam, the extensive train services provided by NS (Nationale Spoorwegen) have you covered. Visit NS's official website to easily discover the most efficient route to reach your ultimate destination.

Zaantheater official website

Bike rental

For a unique experience, consider renting a bike at Zaanse Schans. You can arrange your bike rental online or by giving them a call. This organization has its own dedicated website to facilitate the process. Additionally, if you possess an OV-chipcard, a versatile travel pass, you can conveniently rent a bike from the bicycle lot near the Zaandam Centrum station.
